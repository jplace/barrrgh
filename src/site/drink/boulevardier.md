title: Boulevardier
ingredientTags:
  - rye
  - bourbon
  - campari
  - sweet vermouth
ingredients:
  - 2 ounces rye or bourbon
  - 1 ounce campari
  - 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  - lemon peel (garnish)
recipe: |
  Stir ingredients together in a mixing glass filled with ice, and strain into a glass with ice. Garnish with lemon peel.
