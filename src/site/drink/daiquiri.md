title: Daiquiri
ingredientTags:
  - rum
  - lime juice
  - simple syrup
ingredients:
  - 2 ounces gin
  - ¾ ounce lime juice
  - ¾ ounce simple syrup
  - Lime wheel (for garnish)
recipe: |
  Shake the rum, lime juice and simple syrup with ice for a good while. Strain the cocktail into a coupe glass and garnish with a lime wheel.
