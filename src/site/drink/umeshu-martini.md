title: Umeshu Martini
ingredientTags:
  - gin
  - umeshu
  - dry vermouth
  - orange bitters
ingredients:
  - 1½ ounces London dry gin
  - ¾ ounces umeshu
  - ¾ ounce dry vermouth
  - 1 dash orange bitters
  - Lemon twist (for garnish)
recipe: |
  Stir all liquid ingredients in a mixing glass chocked full of ice. Strain into a martini glass and garnish with lemon twist.
notes: |
  Umeshu can be bought or made. [See instructions here!](https://www.justonecookbook.com/plum-wine/)
