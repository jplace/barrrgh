  - rye

ingredients:
  - 1 ounce rye
  - 1 ounce Campari
  - 1 ounce dry vermouth
  - Lemon twist (for garnish)
recipe: |
  In an old-fashioned glass, place ice cubes. Add the rye, Campari, and vermouth. Stir well. Garnish with a lemon twist.
