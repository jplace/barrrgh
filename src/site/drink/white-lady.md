title: White Lady

  - gin
  - lemon juice

ingredients:
  - 2 ounces gin
  - ¾ ounce lemon juice
  - ¾ ounce orange Curaçao
recipe: |
  Shake gin, lemon juice, and orange Curaçao with ice. Strain drink into glass.
notes: |
  For a fresher flavor, Cher adds ¾ ounce tangerine juice in addition to the lemon juice.
