title: Gold Rush
ingredients:
  - 2 ounces bourbon
  - ¾ ounce fresh lemon juice
  - ¾ ounce honey syrup
recipe: |
  Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add the bourbon, lemon juice, and honey syrup. Shake until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass filled with one large ice cube.
