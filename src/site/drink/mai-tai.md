title: Mai Tai
ingredientTags:
  - light rum
  - gold rum
  - orgeat
  - cointreau
  - lime juice
  - dark rum
  - rum
ingredients:
  - 1 ounce light rum
  - 1 ounce gold rum
  - ½ ounce orgeat syrup
  - ½ ounce cointreau
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - Dark rum (float)
  - Mint sprig (garnish)
recipe: |
  Combine light rum, gold rum, orgeat, cointreau, and lime in a shaker with ice and shake vigorously. Pour over ice. Float an extra ½ ounce dark rum over the top and garnish with mint if desired.
