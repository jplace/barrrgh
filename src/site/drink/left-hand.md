title: Left Hand
ingredientTags:
  - bourbon
  - campari
  - sweet vermouth
  - chocolate bitters
ingredients:
  - 1½ ounces bourbon
  - ¾ ounce campari
  - ¾ ounce sweet vermouth
  - 2 dash chocolate bitters
  - Brandied cherry (for garnish)
recipe: |
  Fill a mixing glass with ice. Add all the liquid ingredients and stir. Strain into a coupe glass. Garnish with a brandied cherry.
