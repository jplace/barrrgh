title: Brandy Alexander

  - heavy cream
  - creme de cacao

ingredients:
  - 1 ounce heavy cream
  - 1 ounce dark creme de cacao
  - 1 ounce brandy
  - Freshly grated nutmeg (for garnish)
recipe: |
  Fill shaker with ice and all ingredients. Shake and strain into a coupe glass. Garnish with nutmeg.
